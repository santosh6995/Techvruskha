Our 1st workshop on error detection and correction was held on 28th sept 2014 at 1.30pm in CSE seminar hall.Our most honourable chief guests are Prof.YJ.Pruthviraj the Deputy Director,Dr.U.Eranna the Principal,DR.R.N kulkarni HOD of CSE and DR.B.M Vidyavathi.

VISSION

The idea behind the overall process is to enhance the technical thinking and learning skills of individuals and share the knowledge of technology to the students community through continuous learning and developing process.

MISSION

To develop a computer science forum using sufficient resources and platform available which helps the students to learn the things visually and improve their programming skills and to update knowledge so they can cope up and compete with the people .

OBJECTIVIES
  1. To improve the technical skills.
  2. To improve the tactical and logical thinking.
  3. To improve the programming knowledge.
ADVANTAGES
  1. The workshop on error detection and correction demonstrates the various types of errors and methods to detect and debug them easily.
  2. By explaining the concept and realism and utility, this workshop helps to achieve two goals i.e.It helps beginners to understand the errors and the second one is it makes them easy to debug.
CONCLUSION

Finally we would like to conclude that students are now familiar with different types of errors.Totally the workshop gives good representation about errors in C and C++.Students suggested to conduct many more technical workshops.
