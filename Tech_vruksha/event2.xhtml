VISSION

To enhance the technical thinking and learning skills of individuals and share the knowledge of technology with the students community through continuous learning and developing process.

MISSION

To enrich the knowledge and skill sets of students by providing value added training about data structures.

OBJECTIVIES
  1. To improve basic techniques of algorithms analysis.
  2. Master the implementation of linked data structures such as linked lists.

  4. Master analyzing problems and writing program solutions.
